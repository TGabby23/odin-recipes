Mexican Rice

Here's a simple and flavorful Mexican rice recipe, perfect as a side for any mexican or other dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 5 red tomatoes
  • 1 cup of cilantro
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 table spoon of chicken broth powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Into the blender add 2 cups of water the red tomatoes, garlic, half onion, chicken broth and some cilantro. blend all together until everything is mix together and turned into pure.
  2. Pour 2 table spoons of olive oil on a pot, and once is hot add the rice to toast untill golden brown
  3. Once the rice is well toasted we would add the tomatoe sauce into. Mix it until the rice is totally covered, and add some salt to taste. omce is well mixed. Cover for around 20 minutes low heat.
  4. Do not uncover unless there's water needed, leave until the water is well suck into the rice. Once the water is. gone is time to enjoy!
